Our History

Yatoha TechHub Solutions was born in 2020 from a passionate vision to bridge the gap between innovation and collaboration in the Nigerian tech ecosystem. Our founders recognized a critical need: while there was no shortage of talent and ideas in Nigeria, there was a disconnect between academic learning, practical skills, and industry needs. The concept of Yatoha emerged as a solution to this challenge. We envisioned a space where learners could gain hands-on experience, where industry experts could share their knowledge, and where innovative solutions could be developed to address real-world problems. From day one, the TechHub has been at the core of our organization. More than just an innovation center, it was designed as a mentorship hub – a place where experienced professionals could guide and nurture emerging talent. This mentor-mentee model quickly became our hallmark, setting us apart in the tech education and development landscape. In our early days, we focused on building a team of experts across various domains of tech solutions development. We brought together specialists in data science, software engineering, product management, graphic design, and more. This multidisciplinary approach allowed us to take on complex projects and deliver comprehensive solutions. As we grew, we expanded our training programs, always with an eye on practical, industry-relevant skills. We developed a project-based curriculum and implemented a one-student-to-tutor model to ensure personalized learning experiences.

Recognizing the power of technology in education, we integrated virtual sessions to enhance our delivery and reach learners across geographical boundaries. Our commitment to bridging the gap between education and industry led us to incorporate mentorship programs, interview preparation, and networking opportunities into our offerings. We understood that technical skills alone were not enough – our learners needed guidance on how to navigate the professional world of tech. In parallel with our educational initiatives, we've continually expanded our solutions development capabilities.

We've established ourselves as experts in digitization and custom software development, helping businesses and organizations solve complex problems and embrace digital transformation. Today, Yatoha TechHub Solutions stands as a testament to our founding vision. We've grown from a small team of passionate tech enthusiasts to a dynamic organization at the forefront of ICT innovation in Nigeria. Our journey continues as we strive to shape the future of technology education and development, nurturing the next generation of tech leaders while delivering cutting-edge solutions to our clients.
